Other people in Ware already getting things done

We're not formally affiliated with any of these. This is just a friendly pointer to other active groups already serving Ware, in case one of them is a better fit, or just as good a way to spend an evening.

Local groups

Community workshop

Ware Men's Shed

Cemetery Lodge, Watton Road, Ware, SG12 0AX · Monday mornings

A registered charity (no. 1194281) giving people — traditionally older men, though many Sheds now welcome a much wider mix — a space to build, fix and restore things alongside each other. The focus is as much on company and conversation as it is on the woodworking and repairs.

Visit waremensshed.org.uk →

Art & craft studio

The Maker & Mind Studio

3 Baldock Street, Ware, SG12 9DH

A textile and fibre-arts studio running weaving, embroidery, felting and natural dyeing workshops, with a deliberate focus on slowing down and the wellbeing side of making with your hands. Open to all levels — you don't need to already be "good at art."

Visit makerandmind.co.uk →

Community service club, est. 1997

Rotary Club of Hertford Shires

Wodson Park Sports & Leisure Centre, Wadesmill Road, Ware, SG12 0UQ · Wednesdays, 7am for 7.15am

A local branch of Rotary International, around 30 members, raising money for local and international causes. Locally that's meant food bank collections, an annual Dragon Boat Race on the Lea, youth competitions for primary schools, and a regular presence at town events like the Ware Dickensian evening.

Visit hertfordshiresrotary.org.uk →

Community gardening, est. 2008

Ware in Bloom

Town centre, Ware · wareinthebloom@gmail.com

A volunteer-run group that's tended Ware's town centre beds and High Street planters since 2008, with results good enough to be nominated as Britain in Bloom finalists. They also run gardening competitions for local schools, nurseries, Scouts and Guides.

Visit wareinbloom.org.uk →
